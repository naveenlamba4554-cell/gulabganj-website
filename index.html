Our Heritage

A Legacy of Atithi Devo Bhava

Located in the heart of Rewari, near the railway station on Circular Road, The Gulab Ganj is a celebration of vegetarian culinary traditions. Our flagship haveli has been a beloved landmark of hospitality, offering families a taste of royal Indian flavors in a regal setting.

The Beginning

Born from a passion for authentic flavors and a desire to bring families together, The Gulab Ganj started as a premium dining experience dedicated to traditional culinary arts.
